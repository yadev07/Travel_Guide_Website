Essential Travel Tips for India

Discover helpful tips for a smooth and enjoyable trip across India!

Packing Essentials

Carry light cotton clothes for the summer, warm layers for winter, and comfortable footwear for all your explorations.

Budget Travel Tips

Use Indian Railways

Indian Railways is a budget-friendly and convenient way to explore the vast country. Consider booking sleeper class for affordable travel.

Book Accommodations in Advance

Save money and avoid last-minute hassles by booking accommodations ahead of time. Use apps and websites for discounted rates.

Enjoy Street Food

Don’t miss the delicious street food! It's cheap, authentic, and part of the real Indian experience. Be cautious about hygiene.

Safety Tips

Avoid Scams

Be cautious about over-friendly strangers offering unsolicited help or tour guides. Stick to well-known services and use your instincts.

Use Official Transport

Opt for official taxis, rideshare apps, or public transportation. Avoid unmarked vehicles or unsafe modes of travel.

Keep Emergency Contacts Handy

Have a list of emergency numbers and contact information for your embassy or consulate in India. Keep these accessible at all times.

Cultural Etiquette

Dress Modestly in Temples

When visiting temples, ensure you dress modestly and respectfully. Cover your shoulders and legs to honor local traditions.

Remove Footwear Before Entering Homes

It is a common practice to remove your shoes before entering someone’s home or religious places. Be mindful of this cultural norm.

Greet with 'Namaste'

'Namaste' is the traditional Indian greeting. Place your hands together in a prayer-like gesture and say "Namaste" as a sign of respect.
